Active Contaminants

    Industrial Waste

    Factories release heavy metals like lead, mercury, and cadmium into the soil through improper disposal. These toxins can persist for centuries.

    • Lead exposure affects 800+ million children globally
    • Heavy metals bioaccumulate in food chains
    • Groundwater contamination spreads pollution for miles

    Pesticides & Herbicides

    Agricultural chemicals designed to kill pests don't discriminate - they destroy beneficial soil organisms essential for healthy ecosystems.

    • 5.6 billion pounds of pesticides used annually worldwide
    • Some compounds persist in soil for 15+ years
    • Kills earthworms, beneficial bacteria, and fungi

    Plastic Pollution

    Plastic breaks down into microplastics that infiltrate every layer of soil, disrupting nutrient cycles and harming soil organisms.

    • Microplastics found in 90% of tested agricultural soils
    • Takes 400+ years to decompose
    • Leaches harmful additives into groundwater

    Mining Activities

    Mining operations expose sulfide minerals that react with air and water, creating acid mine drainage that devastates surrounding ecosystems.

    • Acid drainage can continue for centuries after mines close
    • Destroys aquatic life for miles downstream
    • Heavy metals concentrate in agricultural areas
